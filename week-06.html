Week 6 Philosophy

4 Negotiation Principles

Week 5 dạy bạn professional communication. Week 6 dạy bạn negotiation thinking - framework tư duy để tạo giá trị trong mọi thương lượng. Bốn nguyên tắc này sẽ xuyên suốt 7 ngày và thay đổi cách bạn tiếp cận mọi cuộc đàm phán.

PRINCIPLE 1
Understand before persuading
Hiểu đối phương trước khi thuyết phục họ. Lắng nghe để biết họ thực sự cần gì, không phải họ nói họ cần gì.
PRINCIPLE 2
Interests before positions
Tập trung vào lợi ích thực sự, không phải lập trường bề ngoài. "Tôi muốn tăng lương" vs "Tôi cần được công nhận giá trị".
PRINCIPLE 3
Value before compromise
Tạo giá trị trước khi thương lượng. Đừng chia chiếc bánh - hãy làm chiếc bánh lớn hơn.
PRINCIPLE 4
Commitment before agreement
Cam kết cụ thể trước khi đồng ý chung chung. "Ai làm gì, khi nào, và làm sao biết đã xong?"
Persuasion vs Negotiation Thinking
Persuasion (B2 mindset)
"I want more budget because we need it. This is important for our team. You should approve this."
Negotiation Thinking (C1 mindset)
"Help me understand what's driving your concern about budget. What if we structured this so it delivers ROI in Q3 while addressing your risk concerns? Let's map out who does what by when."

7 Days of Negotiation Thinking

Mỗi ngày xây dựng một khía cạnh của negotiation mindset. Từ hiểu interests đến tạo commitment cụ thể.

DAY 36
Negotiation Mindset & Interests
Understand interests vs positions. Listen to understand, not to respond.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Help me understand what's driving that concern..."
  • "What's most important to you about this?"
  • "If we could solve X, would that address your concern?"
  • "What would success look like for you?"
  • "What's the real challenge here?"
60 min
DAY 37
Building the Business Case
Structure your proposal with cost, benefit, risk, and recommendation.
Key Frameworks:
  • "The investment would be... with ROI of..."
  • "The risk of inaction is..."
  • "Here are the alternatives we considered..."
  • "My recommendation is... because..."
  • "The trade-off is..."
60 min
DAY 38
Handling Objections
Turn objections into opportunities. Address concerns without being defensive.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I understand your concern about..."
  • "That's a valid point. Let me address that..."
  • "What if we approached it this way..."
  • "The data actually shows..."
  • "Here's how we'd mitigate that risk..."
60 min
DAY 39
Stakeholder Mapping & Win-Win
Map stakeholder interests. Create solutions where everyone wins.
Key Frameworks:
  • "What if we structured this so everyone wins?"
  • "Here's how this benefits both of us..."
  • "The key is finding where our interests align..."
  • "What would make this work for you?"
  • "Let's explore options that..."
60 min
DAY 40
Coalition Building
Build alliances across departments. Influence networks, not just individuals.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I'd love your perspective on this..."
  • "Your expertise would be invaluable because..."
  • "This aligns with what you've been working on..."
  • "Would you be open to exploring..."
  • "I'm counting on your support because..."
60 min
DAY 41
Commitment & Follow-through
Get specific commitments. Who does what, by when, and how we'll know it's done.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Based on what we've discussed, the next step would be..."
  • "Are we aligned on moving forward with..."
  • "What would it take to get this approved by..."
  • "Let's commit to [action] by [date]..."
  • "How will we know this is successful?"
60 min
DAY 42
Negotiation Simulation
Full negotiation simulation. Apply all 6 days in one complete negotiation.
Focus:
  • Complete negotiation from start to finish
  • Apply 4 principles in real-time
  • Self-assessment & reflection
  • Celebrate Milestone 6
60 min
