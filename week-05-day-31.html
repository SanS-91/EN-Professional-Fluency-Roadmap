Framing Your Message

Cùng một ý, nhưng nói 4 cách khác nhau tùy context. Hôm nay bạn sẽ học cách frame cùng 1 message theo Casual → Neutral → Professional → More Formal — và biết khi nào dùng cách nào.

Today's Philosophy

Same Idea, Different Frames

Người B2 nói 1 ý theo 1 cách. Người C1 nói cùng 1 ý theo nhiều cách tùy context. Hôm nay bạn sẽ học cách frame cùng 1 message theo 4 registers: Casual → Neutral → Professional → More Formal.

No register is "better" than another. Casual, neutral, and professional are all correct when they match the context. The lesson teaches appropriateness, not hierarchy.

Context quyết định lựa chọn. Đó mới là C1.

Register Mismatch
  • Dùng "Perhaps we should consider..." với bạn → nghe robotic
  • Dùng "We should leave earlier" với sếp → nghe pushy
  • Stuck ở 1 register, không shift linh hoạt
  • Không đọc được context
Register Awareness
  • Đọc được context (who, where, relationship)
  • Adjust register linh hoạt
  • Khi không chắc → chọn neutral (safe choice)
  • Cùng 1 ý, nhưng nói 4 cách khác nhau
💡 Register là Continuum, không phải hierarchy: Casual ←→ Neutral ←→ Professional ←→ More Formal. Không có level nào "tốt hơn" — chỉ có level nào phù hợp hơn với context.

Register Progression 🔊

🏠 Casual
"Maybe we should leave a bit earlier."
Với bạn bè, gia đình, peer
☕ Neutral
"One option might be to leave earlier."
Với đồng nghiệp, networking
💼 Professional
"One approach worth considering is leaving earlier."
Trong meeting, với client
📋 More Formal
"Perhaps we should consider adjusting the schedule slightly."
Với senior leaders, formal settings

Examples 🔊

🏠 Life "We're having fun, but maybe we should leave a bit earlier — I've got an early morning tomorrow."
🏠 Life "The agenda is packed. One option might be to leave earlier so everyone has time to prepare."
💼 Business "Given the tight timeline, one approach worth considering is leaving earlier to ensure quality."
💼 Business "Perhaps we should consider adjusting the schedule slightly to optimize resource allocation."
⚠️ Common mistake:
Dùng "Perhaps we should consider..." với bạn → nghe robotic
Match register với context
Why native speakers dùng:
  • Casual "maybe" = softens suggestion, gives room to disagree
  • Neutral "one option" = presents as possibility, not demand
  • Professional "worth considering" = shows respect for listener's decision
  • More Formal "perhaps" = polite, formal, non-confrontational

Integrated Dialogues

Xem cách register được dùng trong 2 dialogues hoàn chỉnh (10+ lượt). Click 🔊 để nghe.

🏠 Dialogue 1: Planning Weekend (Casual)

Context: Minh và Sarah (bạn bè) đang讨论 cuối tuần. Register: Casual throughout.
Sarah:
"I was thinking we could go hiking this weekend. The weather looks perfect."
Minh:
"That sounds fun! But maybe we should leave a bit earlier — last time we got stuck in traffic."
Sarah:
"Good point. One option might be to leave at 7 AM instead of 9."
Minh:
"Yeah, totally. That gives us more time on the trail."
Sarah:
"Actually, I heard about this new trail that's less crowded. We could try this approach — go there instead of the usual one."
Minh:
"That makes sense. I've been meaning to check it out. Let's give it a shot!"
Sarah:
"Perfect! I'll send you the details tonight."
Minh:
"Great! Oh, one thing we should probably figure out is parking. I heard it gets full."
Sarah:
"Good call. I'll check the parking situation and let you know."
Minh:
"Awesome. Looking forward to it!"
Sarah:
"Me too! See you Saturday!"

Analysis — Register Usage:

  • "maybe we should" = casual suggestion
  • "one option might be" = neutral proposal
  • "yeah, totally" = casual agreement
  • "we could try" = neutral suggestion
  • "that makes sense" = neutral agreement
  • "let's give it a shot" = casual action
  • "should probably figure out" = casual problem-solving
  • Register: Casual throughout (friends)

💼 Dialogue 2: Project Meeting (Professional)

Context: Minh và Lisa (colleagues) đang trong project meeting. Register: Professional → More Formal.
Lisa:
"I've been reviewing the timeline, and I think we need to adjust the launch date."
Minh:
"From my perspective, there are some concerns about the current timeline. The QA phase is too compressed."
Lisa:
"I share that perspective. We're risking quality for speed."
Minh:
"One approach worth considering is pushing the launch by 2 weeks. That gives us proper testing time."
Lisa:
"That makes sense. But we need to communicate this to stakeholders."
Minh:
"I'd suggest we move forward with this approach and prepare a stakeholder update."
Lisa:
"Agreed. This deserves a closer look — let's analyze the impact on other projects."
Minh:
"Good idea. We might want to address this in tomorrow's standup."
Lisa:
"Perfect. I'll draft the communication."
Minh:
"I'm aligned with that view. Let's also consider the budget implications."
Lisa:
"Definitely. I think it's worth moving forward with a detailed analysis."
Minh:
"Great. Let's schedule a follow-up meeting for next week."

Analysis — Register Usage:

  • "from my perspective" = professional opinion
  • "i share that perspective" = professional agreement
  • "one approach worth considering" = professional suggestion
  • "that makes sense" = neutral agreement
  • "i'd suggest we move forward" = professional action
  • "deserves a closer look" = more formal analysis
  • "might want to address" = neutral proposal
  • "aligned with that view" = more formal alignment
  • "worth moving forward" = more formal decision
  • Register: Professional → More Formal (meeting context)
  • 12 lượt hội thoại, thấy register shift

🎵 Intonation Patterns — When & Why

"Maybe we should..." ↗↘ Rise-fall Context: Dùng khi đưa ra suggestion nhẹ nhàng. Rise tạo softness, fall thể hiện suggestion. Tránh: Dùng với urgent matters (nghe quá casual).
"One option might be..." ↘ Falling Context: Dùng khi presents as possibility. Falling tone = presents as option, not demand. Tránh: Rising (nghe như question).
"Worth considering..." ↘ Falling Context: Dùng khi shows importance. Falling tone = shows respect for listener's decision. Tránh: Rising (nghe uncertain).
"From my perspective..." ↘ Falling Context: Dùng khi sharing professional opinion. Falling tone = acknowledges subjectivity. Tránh: Rising (nghe như questioning).
"Let's give it a shot" ↗ Rising Context: Dùng khi enthusiastic, low-pressure. Rising tone = enthusiastic invitation. Tránh: Falling (nghe như command).

Success Criteria

Pro Tips
