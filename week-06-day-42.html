Integration Decoder (Signature Section)

When to use which move during a negotiation. This is your roadmap for applying all 6 days.

🔍 Integration Decoder

Stage of NegotiationBest DayExample

Proposing SolutionDay 37: Persuasive Proposal"Would you be comfortable if I proposed..."

💡 How to use: 1. Identify which stage of negotiation you're in. 2. Choose the day that fits that stage. 3. Apply the moves naturally. 4. Move to the next stage.

Key insight: You don't use all 6 days in every conversation. You use the right day for the right stage.

Scenario 1: Family Budget Discussion

You and your spouse are discussing buying a new car. There are multiple concerns: budget, timing, type of car. Apply all 6 days.

💡 Model Opening: "I'd love to understand what you're hoping for in a new car." 📖 Possible Response: "I'd love to understand what you're hoping for in a new car. So what I'm hearing is you want reliability, I want fuel efficiency, and we both want to stay within budget. Would you be comfortable if I proposed a hybrid? That way we get reliability, fuel efficiency, and it's within our budget. I understand your concern about the upfront cost. What if we looked at leasing instead of buying? That way we get the car now and pay less upfront. Let's commit to researching 3 options by Friday and deciding on Sunday. Does that work?"
