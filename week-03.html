Week 3 Philosophy

From Description to Storytelling

Week 1-2 đã dạy bạn describe (I am..., I have..., I like...). Week 3 sẽ dạy bạn tell stories (You'll never believe..., So there I was..., Looking back...). Đây là sự khác biệt giữa biết tiếng Anh và khiến người khác thích nghe bạn nói.

Storytelling vs Description
Description (IELTS style)
"The most memorable experience was hiking Mount Fuji because the view was breathtaking."
Storytelling (C1 style)
"You'll never believe what happened when we hiked Mount Fuji. So there I was, exhausted at 3 AM, wondering why I signed up for this. But then the sun came up, and it was the most breathtaking thing I've ever seen. The funny thing was that the person I was complaining about being slow ended up being the one who helped me when I struggled."

Key patterns:
  • "You'll never believe what happened when..."
  • "So there I was, [situation]..."
  • "Funny story..."
  • "Long story short..."
  • "The whole time, I was thinking..."
DAY 16

Unexpected Moments

Plot twists, surprises, turning points

Key patterns:
  • "At first, I thought... but it turned out..."
  • "It turned out that..."
  • "Looking back, I realize..."
  • "The funny thing was..."
  • "I wasn't expecting... but..."
DAY 17

People I Met

Character-driven stories, memorable encounters

Key patterns:
  • "I met this [person] who..."
  • "What struck me about them was..."
  • "They told me... and it made me realize..."
  • "We ended up [action] together"
  • "I'll never forget [person] because..."
DAY 18

Food & Culture Stories

Sensory stories, cultural immersion

Key patterns:
  • "I'll never forget the first time I tried..."
  • "The thing about [food/culture] is that..."
  • "What I didn't realize was..."
  • "It reminded me of..."
  • "By the end, I couldn't imagine..."
DAY 19

Lessons Learned

Reflective storytelling, personal growth

Key patterns:
  • "Looking back, I realize..."
  • "What I learned from that was..."
  • "If I could do it again, I would..."
  • "The biggest takeaway was..."
  • "It made me realize that..."
DAY 20

Stories Worth Telling

Polished storytelling, stories worth sharing

Key patterns:
  • "Let me tell you about..."

Storytelling in Action

Xem cách storytelling patterns được dùng trong conversation thực tế. Không phải presentation - mà là conversation.

Day 15: Travel Stories

"You'll never believe what happened when we went to Japan. So there I was, lost in a small town with no phone signal. Funny story — we ended up having dinner with a local family who didn't speak any English, but we communicated through gestures and had the best time. Long story short, we missed our train but ended up having the most memorable experience of the trip. The whole time, I was thinking this was a disaster, but it turned out to be the highlight."

Day 16: Unexpected Moments

"At first, I thought the hiking trip was going to be exhausting, but it turned out to be the most refreshing experience. Looking back, I realize that pushing myself out of my comfort zone was exactly what I needed. The funny thing was that the person I was complaining about being slow ended up being the one who helped me when I struggled. I wasn't expecting to learn so much about myself, but that trip changed my perspective on challenges."

Day 17: People I Met

"I met this elderly woman at the hostel who had been traveling solo for 20 years. What struck me about her was how she could connect with anyone, regardless of language barriers. She told me about her journey through South America, and it made me realize that fear is the only real barrier to travel. We ended up having dinner together and talking for hours about our adventures. I'll never forget her because she showed me that it's never too late to start exploring the world."

1. Storytelling khác description ở điểm nào?

A. Storytelling dài hơn description
B. Storytelling tạo engagement và emotion, description chỉ cung cấp facts
C. Storytelling chỉ dùng trong informal contexts
D. Không có sự khác biệt

2. Pattern nào là storytelling pattern?

A. "The most memorable experience was..."
B. "You'll never believe what happened when..."
C. "I'd recommend visiting..."
D. "The best thing about... was..."

3. Triết lý của Week 3 là gì?

A. Travel is about describing places
B. Travel is about recommending places
C. Travel isn't about describing places. It's about telling stories that people enjoy listening to

4. Câu nào là storytelling C1 style?

B. "The most memorable experience was getting lost in Tokyo because we met interesting people."
C. "I'd recommend getting lost in Tokyo because it's an interesting experience."
D. "The best thing about Tokyo is that you can get lost and meet people."
