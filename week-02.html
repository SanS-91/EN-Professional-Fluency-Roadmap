🌅 Everyday Fluency

Tuần 2 tập trung vào cuộc sống hàng ngày và cách làm việc - những chủ đề giúp bạn build relationships sâu hơn với colleagues, clients, và connections.

People don't connect over job titles. They connect over how they live and work.
🌅 7 days ⏱️ 60 min/day 🏠 50% Life 💼 50% Business 📚 30+ patterns

💡 Week 2 Philosophy

🌅 Từ "First Impressions" đến "Everyday Life"

Tuần 1 bạn đã học cách giới thiệu bản thân. Tuần 2 bạn sẽ học cách nói về cuộc sống - những chủ đề giúp build relationships sâu hơn. Khi ai đó hỏi "What's your daily routine like?" hoặc "What do you do on weekends?", bạn sẽ trả lời với personality, depth, và natural flow.

❌ Vấn đề của người B2

"I wake up at 6 AM. I exercise. I work. I go home."

Nghe như robot. Không có personality. Không có depth. Không có gì để đối phương bám vào.

✅ C1 speakers làm gì khác?

"I'm an early bird — I usually start my day around 5:30 with a 30-minute run, mostly around the lake near my place, but sometimes I switch to yoga when the weather's bad. I've found that morning exercise sets the tone for the whole day."
  • "I'm an early bird" - personality touch
  • "mostly X, but sometimes Y" - nuance (pattern từ tuần 1!)
  • "I've found that..." - insight
  • Specific details: 5:30, 30-minute run
  • Hooks: "morning exercise sets the tone"

📚 30+ Patterns Preview

Tuần 2 sẽ dạy bạn 30+ patterns mới, chia thành 6 categories. Tất cả đều có ví dụ Life & Business.

🌅 Morning & Routines

  • "I'm an early bird / night owl"
  • "I usually start my day with [activity]"
  • "I've found that [habit] sets the tone for..."
  • "The first [time] of my day is for [activity]"
  • "I block out [time] for [activity]"

⚡ Productivity & Flow

  • "What works for me is [system]"
  • "I've been using [method] for [time]"
  • "The key is [principle], not [misconception]"
  • "I struggle with [challenge], so I [solution]"
  • "My system is simple: [1], [2], [3]"

🌴 Weekend & Balance

  • "On weekends, I usually [activity]"
  • "I make sure to [habit] to avoid burnout"
  • "I've set a boundary: [rule]"
  • "The key is [principle], not [approach]"
  • "It helps me [benefit]"

🍳 Food & Lifestyle

  • "I love cooking [dish] — it's my specialty"
  • "I've been trying to [habit] more"
  • "I switched from [X] to [Y] because [reason]"
  • "It's not perfect — [limitation] — but..."
  • "If I could recommend one [thing], it would be..."

🏃 Fitness & Health

  • "I've built a habit of [habit]"
  • "It started when [origin story]"
  • "The system is: [trigger] → [action] → [reward]"
  • "Consistency matters more than [belief]"
  • "The biggest lesson I've learned is [insight]"

💬 Response & Empathy

  • "That makes sense."
  • "I've had the same experience."
  • "I'm still figuring that out."
  • "It depends on the situation."
  • "I see what you mean, but..."

🔗 Patterns từ tuần 1 sẽ được áp dụng:

"Mostly X, but sometimes Y" "I usually run in the morning, mostly around the lake, but sometimes I switch to yoga"
"Actually, [elaboration]" "I'm an early bird. Actually, I've been waking up at 5:30 for the past 2 years"
"That reminds me of..." "I use Notion for everything. That reminds me of a colleague who swears by Obsidian"
Specific details "I've been using Todoist for 3 years, and it's helped me close 50+ projects"
