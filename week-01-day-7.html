🗺️ Week 1 Recap - Mind Map

Tổng hợp TẤT CẢ patterns từ 6 ngày. Đây là "vũ khí" của bạn - hãy ôn lại để không quên.

🏆 WEEK 1: FIRST IMPRESSIONS & IDENTITY
1
Natural Introductions
  • "I'm based in X, but originally from Y"
  • "I've been [verb+ing] for a while now"
  • "I'm into X — mostly [detail]"
  • "It's been on my list to [verb]"
  • "Great to finally meet you in person"
  • "We should definitely [verb] sometime"
2
Register Shifting
  • Work: "I work in" → "I'm in" → "I work as"
  • Hobbies: "I'm into" → "big fan of" → "strong interest"
  • Time: "for a while" → "for X years" → "I bring X years"
  • Interest: "awesome!" → "fascinating" → "compelling"
  • Follow-up: "grab coffee" → "connect again" → "welcome opportunity"
  • Exit: "I'll let you go" → "great connecting" → "thank you"
3
Conversation Extension
  • "Actually, [elaboration with surprise]"
  • "Mostly X, but sometimes Y"
  • "That reminds me of..."
  • "What about you? How's [specific] going?"
  • "Speaking of [topic], ..."
  • "Oh, before I forget, ..."
4
Listening Deep Dive
  • Linking (C-V): "based in" → "basedin"
  • Intrusion: "go out" → "go-W-out"
  • Elision: "next day" → "nex day"
  • Assimilation: "did you" → "did-ju"
  • Reduction: "to" → "tə", "and" → "n"
  • Contractions: "gonna", "wanna", "gotta"
5
Speaking Intensive
  • Content (20%) - structure, hooks
  • Pronunciation (20%) - /θ/, /ð/, stress
  • Intonation (15%) - rising/falling
  • Pace & Pauses (15%) - 130-150 WPM
  • Fluency (15%) - <5 fillers/min
  • Naturalness (15%) - connected speech
6
LinkedIn Profile
  • Hook Opener (story/question/bold)
  • "Mostly X, but sometimes Y"
  • Specific Details (numbers, names)
  • Value Proposition
  • Call to Action
  • Personality Touch

🔑 10 Key Takeaways

1
C1 ≠ Fancy vocabulary C1 = Natural + Precise + Context-aware
2
Register shifting Biết dùng ĐÚNG từ, ĐÚNG lúc, với ĐÚNG người
3
Hooks tạo curiosity gap Khiến reader/listener muốn tiếp tục
4
Specific details = credibility Numbers, names, places > vague statements
5
Connected speech Native nói thành dòng chảy, không từng từ
6
Record → Listen → Improve Không hear lại giọng mình = không tiến bộ
7
3 iterations framework Content → Delivery → Naturalness
8
Writing = Speaking on paper Tất cả patterns đều apply được
9
5-paragraph structure Hook → What → Why → Value → CTA
10
Review day = permanent learning Không review = quên 70% sau 1 tuần

🔄 Integration Challenge: 5-Minute Conversation

Dùng ít nhất 15 patterns từ Week 1 trong 1 conversation hoàn chỉnh. Đây là "final exam" của tuần 1.

🎯 Context: Bạn đang ở networking event. Gặp Lisa (professional khác) ở coffee station. Conversation kéo dài 5 phút.

💬 Sample Conversation Script

Đọc script này để thấy cách TẤT CẢ patterns được integrate. Highlighted phrases = patterns from Week 1.

YOU: Hi, I don't believe we've met. I'm Minh Nguyen.
LISA: Hi Minh! I'm Lisa Tran from Saigon Capital. Great to finally meet you in person — I've been following Horizon Partners' work.
YOU: Oh really? That's fascinating! I'm based in Saigon, but originally from Hanoi. I've been in real estate PE for about 8 years now, mostly logistics, but sometimes industrial properties when numbers make sense.
LISA: That's interesting! We're actually looking at logistics space too.
YOU: Actually, we just closed a series A with Vietnam's fastest-growing last-mile delivery startup. That reminds me of a similar company we looked at last year — they got acquired by Shopee.
LISA: No way! What was your thesis there?
YOU: Mostly e-commerce growth, but sometimes we bet on infrastructure plays. Speaking of which, what about your firm? How's the fundraising going for Fund III?
LISA: Pretty well, thanks. We're about 60% subscribed.
YOU: That's great! Oh, before I forget, I have a friend who runs a family office in Singapore — they've been looking at Vietnam-focused funds. Would you be open to an intro?
LISA: Absolutely! That would be amazing.
YOU: Perfect. Let's definitely connect again soon — maybe grab coffee next week to discuss further?
LISA: Sounds great! Here's my card.
YOU: It was great connecting with you, Lisa. Have a great rest of the conference!

🎯 Reality Task - Choose Your Level

Bài tập THỰC TẾ - apply tất cả patterns vào đời thật. Chọn 1 trong 3 levels tùy thời gian và comfort level.

EASY

📝 LinkedIn About Section

⏱️ 15 minutes

Viết hoặc rewrite LinkedIn About section hoàn chỉnh (1200-1500 characters).

Requirements:
  • Apply TẤT CẢ 6 writing patterns từ Day 6
  • Structure 5 paragraphs
  • Có ít nhất 3 specific details
  • Balance professional + personality
  • Length: 1200-1500 characters
Deliverable: Screenshot LinkedIn About section đã publish
STANDARD

🤝 Real-Life Introduction

⏱️ 30 minutes

Đi đến 1 nơi công cộng (café, co-working, event) và introduce yourself với 1 người lạ bằng tiếng Anh.

Requirements:
  • Dùng ít nhất 10 patterns từ Week 1
  • Conversation kéo dài 3-5 phút
  • Record conversation (audio hoặc notes)
  • Self-assess sau conversation
Deliverable: Audio recording HOẶC written notes
CHALLENGE

🎥 Video Pitch

⏱️ 60 minutes

Record 60-second video pitch giới thiệu bản thân và work. Upload lên LinkedIn hoặc gửi cho bạn để get feedback.

Requirements:
  • Video 60 giây (không dài hơn)
  • Apply TẤT CẢ patterns từ Week 1
  • Professional nhưng vẫn có personality
  • Có hook, specifics, value prop, CTA
  • Record 3 iterations
Deliverable: Video file HOẶC LinkedIn post với video
