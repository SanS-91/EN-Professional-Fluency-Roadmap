Week 7 Philosophy

4 Leadership Principles

Week 5 dạy bạn professional communication. Week 6 dạy bạn negotiation thinking. Week 7 dạy bạn leadership communication - framework tư duy để truyền cảm hứng và dẫn dắt người khác. Bốn nguyên tắc này sẽ xuyên suốt 7 ngày và thay đổi cách bạn dẫn dắt mọi cuộc trò chuyện.

PRINCIPLE 1
Vision before tasks
Truyền tầm nhìn trước khi giao việc. People don't follow tasks, they follow visions.
PRINCIPLE 2
Purpose before what
Giải thích ý nghĩa trước khi nói việc gì. When people understand why, they commit to what.
PRINCIPLE 3
Listening before answers
Lắng nghe & đặt câu hỏi trước khi đưa giải pháp. Great leaders ask better questions than they give answers.
PRINCIPLE 4
Recognition before correction
Khen ngợi trước khi điều chỉnh. Recognition builds the trust that makes correction possible.
Manager vs Leader Communication
Manager (Task-focused)
"Did you finish X? What about Y? We need to hit the deadline. Here's what you should do."
Leader (People-focused)
"Imagine where we'll be in 6 months if we nail this. How are you experiencing this challenge? What support do you need from me? I noticed your contribution made a real difference."

7 Days of Leadership Communication

Mỗi ngày xây dựng một khía cạnh của leadership mindset. Từ truyền vision đến xử lý difficult conversations.

DAY 43
Vision Casting & Inspiration
Truyền vision để team thấy purpose. First team meeting sau khi được promote.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Imagine where we'll be in [timeframe]..."
  • "This is bigger than just [task]..."
  • "I see a future where..."
  • "What excites me about this is..."
  • "Together, we can..."
60 min
DAY 44
Communicating Purpose
Giải thích purpose cho project mới. Khi people understand why, họ commit to what.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Here's why this matters..."
  • "The reason we're doing this is..."
  • "What's at stake if we don't..."
  • "This connects to our larger goal of..."
  • "I want to be transparent about..."
60 min
DAY 45
Active Listening & Powerful Questions
1-on-1 để hiểu root cause. Framework: Reflect → Clarify → Ask → Summarize.
Key Frameworks:
  • "What do you think is driving this?"
  • "How are you experiencing this?"
  • "What would success look like for you?"
  • "What's getting in the way?"
  • "What support do you need from me?"
60 min
DAY 46
Recognition & Appreciation
Team meeting để recognize contributions cụ thể. Build morale và trust.
Key Frameworks:
  • "What I noticed about [Name]'s work was..."
  • "The impact of [action] was..."
  • "I want to acknowledge..."
  • "This exemplifies our value of..."
  • "Thank you for..."
60 min
DAY 47
Addressing Difficult Conversations
Xử lý performance, conflict, misunderstanding, broken commitment với care.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I've noticed [behavior], and I want to understand..."
  • "Help me understand what's going on..."
  • "Here's the impact I'm seeing..."
  • "What support would help you succeed?"
  • "Let's create a plan together..."
60 min
DAY 48
Communicating Difficult Decisions
Communicate budget cut, restructure, no promotion, project cancelled với clarity và care.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I have a difficult decision to share..."
  • "Here's the context behind this..."
  • "I understand this may be concerning..."
  • "Here's what this means for you..."
  • "I'm committed to supporting you through..."
60 min
DAY 49
Leadership Simulation
Mô phỏng toàn bộ leadership journey. Apply all 6 days trong one complete simulation.
Focus:
  • Complete leadership journey simulation
  • Apply 4 principles in real-time
  • Self-assessment & reflection
  • Celebrate Milestone 7
60 min
