Relationship Builder

Week 4 không phải về gặp nhiều người. Week 4 là về khiến người khác cảm thấy được thấy, được nghe, và được nhớ. Networking chỉ là bối cảnh - connection mới là điều quan trọng.

Great networking isn't about meeting more people. It's about making people feel seen, heard, and remembered.
Phase 1
7 days × 60 min
50% Life
50% Business
30 relationship patterns

Week 4 Philosophy

From Transaction to Connection

Week 1-3 đã dạy bạn introduce, describe, và tell stories. Week 4 sẽ dạy bạn build relationships - khiến người khác cảm thấy seen, heard, and remembered. Đây là sự khác biệt giữa biết tiếng Anh và khiến người khác muốn kết nối với bạn.

Small Talk vs Relationship Building
Small Talk (Surface level)
"Nice to meet you. What do you do? Oh, interesting. Here's my card."
Relationship Building (C1 style)
"You know, I've been following your work on sustainable design. What got you interested in that field? That's fascinating — I'd love to hear more about your recent project. Let's grab coffee next week and continue this conversation."

7 Days of Relationship Building

Mỗi ngày tập trung vào 1 khía cạnh của việc xây dựng connection. Không phải networking - mà là relationship.

DAY 22

Curiosity & Warmth

Ask questions people enjoy answering. Create comfortable spaces for genuine conversation.

Key Patterns:
  • "What got you interested in [field]?"
  • "What's been the most rewarding part?"
  • "What's something people misunderstand about...?"
  • "If you had to start over, would you...?"
  • "What question do you wish more people asked?"
60 min
DAY 23

Genuine Connection

Share authentically to invite relationship. Move from transactional to relational.

Key Patterns:
  • "That actually reminds me of..."
  • "I've been thinking about that a lot lately..."
  • "You know what's interesting about that?"
  • "I have a friend who does something similar..."
  • "I'd love to hear more about that..."
60 min
DAY 24

Empathy & Reading the Room

Listen deeply, read social cues. Know when to lean in, step back, or change direction.

Key Patterns:
  • "That sounds really [emotion]..."
  • "I can tell this really matters to you..."
  • "What was that like for you?"
  • "I can see why you'd feel that way..."
  • "That must have been [challenging/exciting]..."
60 min
DAY 25

Graceful Exits

End conversations so people want to meet you again, not feel relieved it's over.

Key Patterns:
  • "I've really enjoyed this conversation..."
  • "I don't want to monopolize your time..."
  • "Before I let you go, I'd love to..."
  • "This has given me a lot to think about..."
  • "I'd love to continue this over coffee..."
60 min
DAY 26

Nurturing Relationships

Maintain connections through consistent care. Digital follow-up mastery.

Key Patterns:
  • "This reminded me of our conversation about..."
  • "I came across this and thought of you..."
  • "I've been thinking about what you said..."
  • "I'd love to introduce you to..."
  • "No need to reply, just wanted you to know..."
60 min
DAY 27

Connection Through Experiences

Build bonds through shared moments - meals, activities, and memorable experiences.

Key Patterns:
  • "I'd love to experience [activity] with you..."
  • "Have you ever tried [experience]?"
  • "This reminds me of when we [shared moment]..."
  • "Let's make this a tradition..."
  • "I'm so glad we did this..."
60 min
DAY 28

Week 4 Review

Review 30 patterns, integrated practice, celebrate Milestone 4.

Focus:
  • Review all 30 relationship patterns
  • Integrated relationship practice
  • Self-assessment & reflection
  • Celebrate Milestone 4
60 min
