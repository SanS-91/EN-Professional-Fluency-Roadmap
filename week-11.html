Week 11 Philosophy

4 Conflict Resolution Principles

Week 5-10 đã dạy bạn **communicate professionally**. Week 11 dạy bạn **handle what breaks** - framework tư duy để navigate emotional tension và repair relationships. Bốn nguyên tắc này sẽ xuyên suốt 7 ngày và thay đổi cách bạn đối mặt với conflict.

PRINCIPLE 1
Emotion before logic
Handle feelings first, then facts. People can't hear logic until they feel heard.
PRINCIPLE 2
Curiosity before judgment
Understand before you respond. Assume positive intent until proven otherwise.
PRINCIPLE 3
Repair before resolve
Restore trust first, then solve the problem. Without repair, solutions don't stick.
PRINCIPLE 4
Forward before backward
Focus on "what do we do now?" not "whose fault is this?"
⚖️ Week 6 vs Week 11: Two Different Competencies

Nhiều người nhầm lẫn Negotiation và Conflict Resolution. Đây là hai kỹ năng hoàn toàn khác nhau.

Week 6: Negotiation
  • Core issue: Competing interests
  • Goal: Create value
  • Approach: Rational, strategic
  • Key question: "How do we both win?"
  • Success metric: Agreement reached
Week 11: Conflict Resolution
  • Core issue: Emotional tension
  • Goal: Restore relationship
  • Approach: Emotional, relational
  • Key question: "How do we repair what's broken?"
  • Success metric: Trust restored

Conflict không phải là vấn đề cần tránh. Conflict là tín hiệu cho thấy điều gì đó quan trọng cần được chú ý.

Reactive vs Strategic Conflict Response
Reactive (B2 approach)
"Let's just pretend this didn't happen." OR "This is all your fault!" OR "I don't want to get involved."
Strategic (C1 approach)
"Conflict tells me something important is at stake. Let me understand what. I can see this is frustrating - let's take a step back and figure this out together."

7 Days of Conflict Resolution

Mỗi ngày xây dựng một khía cạnh của conflict resolution. Từ understanding dynamics đến transforming conflict thành opportunity.

DAY 71
Understanding Conflict Dynamics
Hiểu tại sao conflict xảy ra và các loại conflict khác nhau. Conflict is signal, not problem.
Key Frameworks:
  • "What's really at stake here?"
  • "Is this a task conflict or a relationship conflict?"
  • "What need is not being met?"
  • "The conflict iceberg: surface vs underlying"
  • "When conflict is healthy vs destructive"
60 min
DAY 72
De-escalation & Emotional Regulation
Giữ bình tĩnh và hạ nhiệt tình huống trước khi giải quyết. Handle feelings before facts.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Let's take a breath and approach this calmly"
  • "I can see this matters a lot to you"
  • "Help me understand what's happening from your perspective"
  • "Let's take 10 minutes and come back to this"
  • "Recognize emotional triggers (yours and theirs)"
60 min
DAY 73
The Difficult Conversation Framework
Cấu trúc một cuộc hội thoại khó từ đầu đến cuối. Observation → Inquiry → Acknowledgment → Solution.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I've noticed [behavior], and I want to understand..."
  • "Help me see this from your perspective"
  • "What I'm hearing is... is that right?"
  • "How can we move forward together?"
  • "Opening → Inquiry → Acknowledgment → Problem-solving → Closing"
60 min
DAY 74
Delivering Hard News
Communicate bad news với clarity và compassion. Lead with context, not conclusion.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I have something difficult to share with you"
  • "Here's the context behind this decision"
  • "I understand this is hard to hear"
  • "Here's how I'm going to support you"
  • "Balance honesty with compassion"
60 min
DAY 75
Repairing Broken Trust
Sau conflict, làm sao rebuild trust đã mất. Acknowledge impact, not just intent.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I want to acknowledge the impact of what happened"
  • "I understand now that my actions caused..."
  • "Here's what I'll do differently going forward"
  • "What would help rebuild your trust?"
  • "The anatomy of a genuine apology"
60 min
DAY 76
Mediating Between Others
Đóng vai trò third-party để help hai bên resolve conflict. Stay neutral, facilitate understanding.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Help me understand what's important to each of you"
  • "What I'm hearing from [Name] is..."
  • "Is there something you both agree on?"
  • "What would a good outcome look like for both of you?"
  • "Stay neutral, don't take sides"
60 min
DAY 77
Week 11 Review - The Conflict Navigator
Mô phỏng toàn bộ conflict resolution journey. Transform conflict thành opportunity.
Focus:
  • Complete conflict resolution simulation
  • Apply 4 principles in real-time
  • Self-assessment: How do I respond to conflict?
  • Celebrate Milestone 11
60 min
