👋 First Impressions & Identity

Master introducing yourself in casual and professional settings. Learn natural collocations and adapt your language for any situation.

🌱 Phase 1 ⏱️ 7 days × 60 min 🏠 50% Life 💼 50% Business

🎯 This Week

Learn to introduce yourself confidently in both casual and professional settings. Master 12 natural collocations, understand register differences, and create your elevator pitch.

🏠 Life Goal

Introduce yourself naturally at social events, make small talk, and build rapport with new people.

💼 Business Goal

Deliver a polished 60-second elevator pitch for networking events and professional conferences.
