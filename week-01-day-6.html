✍️ LinkedIn Profile: Writing with Impact

Writing là speaking trên giấy. Hôm nay bạn sẽ apply TẤT CẢ patterns từ Day 1-5 vào LinkedIn About section - sản phẩm thực tế có thể publish ngay.

✍️ Writing Intensive ⏱️ 60 minutes 📚 6 writing patterns 🎯 Real product: LinkedIn profile

💡 Today's Philosophy

✍️ Writing = Speaking on paper

Tất cả patterns từ Day 1-5 đều apply được vào writing. Hook opener (Day 3), "mostly X, but sometimes Y" (Day 3), specific details (Day 4), register balance (Day 2) - tất cả đều dùng được. Hôm nay bạn sẽ viết LinkedIn About section hoàn chỉnh.

❌ Tại sao LinkedIn profile của bạn không ai đọc?

Trung bình recruiter dành 6 giây để scan LinkedIn profile. Nếu không có hook trong 2 giây đầu → họ scroll qua.

❌ B2 Version: "I am an Investment Director with 8 years of experience in real estate private equity. I have a proven track record of successful investments."

5 lỗi phổ biến của người Việt:

  • Generic opener: "I am a hardworking professional..." - nghe như 10,000 profiles khác
  • List skills thay vì tell story: "Skills: Excel, PowerPoint, Teamwork..." - boring, không có impact
  • Quá formal hoặc quá casual: "seasoned professional" (robotic) vs "Hey there!" (unprofessional)
  • Không có hooks: No story, no specific numbers, no personality
  • Không có Call to Action: Reader không biết nên làm gì tiếp

✅ C1 writers làm gì khác?

Họ viết như đang NÓI CHUYỆN với reader:

✅ C1 Version: "Five years ago, I made a bet that Southeast Asia's logistics sector would transform. That bet led me to close 3 series A deals, including one with Vietnam's fastest-growing last-mile delivery startup. Today, I'm still placing those bets — mostly in logistics, but sometimes in industrial properties when the numbers make sense."

Sự khác biệt:

  • Story opener: "Five years ago, I made a bet..."
  • Specific details: 3 deals, Vietnam's fastest-growing
  • "Mostly X, but sometimes Y" pattern (từ Day 3!)
  • Personality: "when the numbers make sense"
  • Conversational tone - viết như đang nói

📚 6 Writing Patterns for LinkedIn

6 patterns để biến LinkedIn profile từ boring → compelling. Áp dụng tất cả patterns từ Day 1-5 vào writing.

1
The Hook Opener

Câu đầu tiên khiến reader phải đọc câu thứ 2. Quyết định reader có tiếp tục đọc hay scroll qua.

Why C1? B2 writers bắt đầu với "I am..." (boring). C1 writers bắt đầu với STORY, QUESTION, hoặc BOLD STATEMENT. Hook tạo curiosity gap → reader phải đọc tiếp.
3 loại Hook
A. Story Hook "Five years ago, I made a bet that..."
"It started with a 2 AM phone call from..."
"When I first entered the industry, everyone told me..."
→ Tạo narrative, reader muốn biết kết quả
B. Question Hook "What if I told you that 80% of logistics startups fail in year 1?"
"Have you ever wondered why Southeast Asia's e-commerce is growing 25% annually?"
"What does a $50M fund have in common with a street food stall?"
→ Tạo curiosity, reader muốn biết câu trả lời
C. Bold Statement Hook "Most real estate funds are playing it safe. We're not."
"I've turned down more deals than I've closed. And I sleep well at night."
"The future of logistics in Southeast Asia isn't what you think."
→ Tạo contrast, reader muốn biết tại sao
⚠️ Common mistake: ❌ Bắt đầu với "I am a..." → ✅ Bắt đầu với story/question/statement
2
"Mostly X, but sometimes Y" (Elaboration)

Elaborate về work với nuance - không chỉ 1 chiều. Apply pattern từ Day 3 vào writing.

Why quan trọng trong writing? Thể hiện depth và complexity. Tạo 2 hooks để reader bám vào. Nghe authentic, không robotic.
Examples
🏢 Work focus "I'm mostly focused on logistics investments, but sometimes we look at industrial properties when the numbers make sense."
🎯 Geographic focus "We invest mostly in Vietnam and Thailand, but sometimes we explore opportunities in Indonesia if they're compelling enough."
💡 Stage focus "I work mostly with series A startups, but sometimes we co-invest in seed rounds when the team is exceptional."
💡 C1 Tip: Dùng pattern này sau khi đã có hook. Đừng dùng ngay câu đầu tiên.
3
The Specific Detail

Numbers, names, places - khiến writing trở nên concrete. Specific details = credibility.

Why quan trọng? "I've closed many deals" → vague. "I've closed 12 deals worth $200M+" → specific, memorable. Mỗi paragraph nên có ít nhất 1 specific detail.
4 Types of Specific Details
A. Numbers "I've closed 12 deals worth $200M+"
"Our fund is up 18% YTD"
"We grew the portfolio 3x in 2 years"
B. Names "Including our series A with [startup name]"
"Working with teams from Shopee, Grab, and TikTok"
C. Places "Across Vietnam, Thailand, and Indonesia"
"From Ho Chi Minh City to Singapore"
D. Time "Over the past 8 years"
"In the last 18 months"
"Since 2018"
❌ Vague "I have extensive experience in real estate."
✅ Specific "Over the past 8 years, I've closed 12 real estate deals worth $200M+ across Vietnam and Thailand."
4
The Value Proposition

Bạn mang lại giá trị gì cho connections? Trả lời câu hỏi: "Why should I connect with this person?"

Why quan trọng? Reader tự hỏi: "Why should I connect with this person?" Value proposition trả lời câu hỏi đó. Khiến connection request có mục đích.
Structure
"I help [target audience] achieve [specific outcome] through [your unique approach]."
Examples
🏢 Investment focus "I help founders in Southeast Asia's logistics sector raise their series A by connecting them with the right investors at the right time."
🎯 Advisory focus "I help real estate funds navigate the Vietnamese market by providing on-the-ground insights that you can't get from a report."
💡 Mentorship focus "I help young professionals in finance build their careers by sharing the lessons I've learned over 8 years in the industry."
💡 C1 Tip: Value proposition nên cụ thể, không generic. "I help people succeed" = quá vague.
5
The Call to Action (CTA)

Reader nên làm gì sau khi đọc profile? Biến passive reader thành active connection.

Why quan trọng? Không có CTA = reader đọc xong rồi đi, không action. CTA biến passive reader thành active connection. Khiến profile có mục đích.
3 Types of CTA
A. Connection CTA "Let's connect if you're in the logistics space in Southeast Asia."
"Reach out if you're a founder raising a series A in Vietnam."
"Happy to connect with fellow real estate investors."
B. Conversation CTA "Always happy to chat about logistics trends over coffee."
"DM me if you want to discuss the future of e-commerce in Southeast Asia."
"Let's grab coffee if you're in Ho Chi Minh City."
C. Resource CTA "I write a weekly newsletter on Southeast Asia logistics - link below."
"Check out my latest article on the future of last-mile delivery."
"Download my free guide on raising series A in Vietnam."
💡 C1 Tip: CTA nên specific và actionable. "Let's connect" quá generic. "Let's connect if you're [specific criteria]" tốt hơn.
6
The Personality Touch

Thêm personality để profile không nghe robotic. Balance: professional + memorable.

Why quan trọng? 100% professional = robotic, không memorable. 100% personal = unprofessional. Balance = memorable + professional.
3 Types of Personality Touch
A. Hobby mention "When I'm not analyzing deals, you'll find me trail running in Da Lat."
"Outside of work, I'm mostly into street photography, but sometimes I do portraits for friends."
"I'm a coffee enthusiast - always looking for the next great café in Saigon."
B. Value statement "I believe the best investments are made when you understand the founders' vision, not just the numbers."
"My philosophy: Play the long game. Short-term wins don't matter if you lose trust."
"I'm passionate about democratizing access to capital for Southeast Asian founders."
C. Quirky detail "Fun fact: I've run 5 marathons, including one in the Sahara Desert."
"I can quote most of The Big Lebowski. Don't ask why."
"My morning routine includes 30 minutes of reading and exactly 2 cups of coffee."
💡 C1 Tip: Personality touch nên ở CUỐI About section, sau value proposition. Không nên ở đầu.

📋 4 Sections of LinkedIn Profile

LinkedIn profile có 4 sections chính. Mỗi section có mục đích và structure riêng.

🎯
Section 1: Headline
120 characters max · First thing people see

Headline nên hook reader. Structure: "[Role] | [Specialty] | [Unique value]"

❌ Generic "Investment Director at XYZ Capital"
✅ Specific + Hook "Investment Director | Real Estate PE | Helping SE Asia logistics founders raise series A"
❌ Boring "Finance professional with 8 years experience"
✅ Story "Investment Director | Closed 12 deals worth $200M+ | Betting on SE Asia's logistics future"
🎯 Target: 80-120 characters, specific, hook-y. Reader nên biết bạn là ai và bạn offer gì trong 3 giây.
📝
Section 2: About Section
1200-1500 characters ideal · Tell your story

About section là nơi bạn tell story, show personality, create hooks. Structure: 5 paragraphs.

5-Paragraph Structure
Paragraph 1: Hook Opener (Pattern 1) - Story, question, or bold statement Paragraph 2: What you do (Pattern 2 + 3) - "Mostly X, but sometimes Y" + specific details Paragraph 3: Why you do it - Your philosophy, what drives you Paragraph 4: Value Proposition (Pattern 4) - What you offer to connections Paragraph 5: CTA + Personality (Pattern 5 + 6) - Call to action + personality touch
💼
Section 3: Experience Descriptions
Show impact, not just responsibilities

Structure: Role + Company + Duration → 1-sentence overview → 3-5 bullet points với NUMBERS → 1 sentence về pride.

Structure
Investment Director | Horizon Partners | 2018 - Present Leading real estate private equity investments across Southeast Asia, with a focus on logistics and industrial properties. • Closed 12 deals worth $200M+ across Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia • Grew portfolio 3x in 2 years, from $50M to $150M AUM • Led series A investment in [startup name], now valued at 5x • Built team of 5 analysts covering 3 countries • Fund performance: 18% YTD, outperforming benchmark by 4% Most proud of: Building relationships with founders that go beyond just capital - many come to us for strategic advice, not just money.
💡 C1 Tip: Mỗi bullet point nên có NUMBER. "Managed team" = vague. "Built team of 5 analysts" = specific.
🎯
Section 4: Skills & Endorsements
SEO + credibility · Add 10-15 skills

Top 3 skills (most visible) nên match với About section. Skills nên specific, không generic.

Top 3 Skills (Most Visible)
1. Real Estate Private Equity 2. Investment Analysis 3. Southeast Asia Markets
Other Skills (Add 10-15 total)
• Financial Modeling • Due Diligence • Deal Sourcing • Portfolio Management • Team Leadership • Logistics • Venture Capital • Startup Advisory • Cross-border Investments • Strategic Planning
💡 C1 Tip: Skills nên match với About section. Nếu About nói về logistics, skills nên có "Logistics", "Supply Chain", etc.

✨ Perfect Example: Full About Section

Đây là ví dụ hoàn chỉnh áp dụng TẤT CẢ 6 patterns. Note cách mỗi paragraph có mục đích riêng.

Five years ago, I made a bet that Southeast Asia's logistics sector would transform. That bet led me to close 3 series A deals, including one with Vietnam's fastest-growing last-mile delivery startup. Today, I'm still placing those bets — mostly in logistics, but sometimes in industrial properties when the numbers make sense. I'm an Investment Director at Horizon Partners, where I've spent the past 8 years building our real estate private equity platform. We've closed 12 deals worth $200M+ across Vietnam, Thailand, and Indonesia. Our fund is up 18% YTD, mostly driven by our logistics portfolio. I believe the best investments are made when you understand the founders' vision, not just the numbers. That's why I spend as much time with founders as I do with spreadsheets. I help logistics founders in Southeast Asia raise their series A by connecting them with the right investors at the right time. If you're building the future of last-mile delivery, warehouse automation, or cross-border logistics, let's talk. When I'm not analyzing deals, you'll find me trail running in Da Lat or experimenting with pour-over coffee. Always happy to chat about logistics trends over a cup — DM me!
📊 ~1,450 characters (perfect length) ✅ All 6 patterns applied
Pattern Breakdown
Paragraph 1: Hook (Story) + "mostly X, but sometimes Y" Paragraph 2: Specific details (12 deals, $200M+, 8 years, 3 countries, 18% YTD) Paragraph 3: Philosophy/Why ("I believe...") Paragraph 4: Value Proposition ("I help logistics founders...") Paragraph 5: Personality (trail running, coffee) + CTA ("DM me!")

🔄 3 Iterations Framework for Writing

Mỗi iteration focus vào 1 aspect khác nhau. Sau 3 iterations, bạn sẽ có LinkedIn profile hoàn chỉnh.

1
Content Focus
15 minutes
Goal: Structure rõ ràng, có hooks, specific details, value proposition, CTA.
Focus areas
Hook opener Specific details Value proposition Call to action
Process
1️⃣ Draft 5 paragraphs: Theo 5-paragraph structure (10 phút)
2️⃣ Self-assess: Có đủ hooks không? Có specific details không? (5 phút)
2
Tone & Register Focus
10 minutes
Goal: Balance professional + personality. Conversational tone. Viết như đang nói.
Focus areas
Register balance Conversational tone Personality touch
Process
1️⃣ Read aloud: Nghe có natural không? (3 phút)
2️⃣ Adjust tone: Thêm personality, giảm robotic phrases (7 phút)
3
Polish Focus
10 minutes
Goal: Grammar, flow, length, formatting. Final read để đảm bảo perfect.
Focus areas
Grammar & spelling Flow Length (1200-1500 chars) Formatting
Process
1️⃣ Check grammar, spelling (3 phút)
2️⃣ Check flow: Transitions OK không? (3 phút)
3️⃣ Check length: Trong target range không? (2 phút)
4️⃣ Final read: Would YOU connect với người này? (2 phút)

✍️ 4 Writing Scenarios

Progression từ write from scratch → rewrite existing → experience description → full profile overhaul.

📝 From Scratch - 35 min

Scenario 1: Write About Section

Bạn chưa có LinkedIn profile hoặc muốn viết lại từ đầu. Apply 6 patterns để viết About section hoàn chỉnh.

Yêu cầu C1:
  • Dùng TẤT CẢ 6 patterns
  • Structure 5 paragraphs
  • Length: 1200-1500 characters
  • Có ít nhất 3 specific details
  • Balance professional + personality
🔄 Rewrite - 35 min

Scenario 2: Rewrite Existing About

Bạn đã có About section nhưng nó generic, boring. Identify weaknesses và apply 6 patterns để cải thiện.

Yêu cầu C1:
  • Identify weaknesses trong current version
  • Apply 6 patterns để cải thiện
  • Keep core message, improve delivery
  • Target: Improve score ≥1.5 points
💼 Experience - 20 min

Scenario 3: Write Experience Description

Viết description cho 1 role trong Experience section. Focus vào impact, không phải responsibilities.

Yêu cầu C1:
  • 1-sentence overview
  • 3-5 bullet points với NUMBERS
  • 1 sentence về pride/achievement
  • Specific, impact-focused
🔥 Challenge - 50 min

Scenario 4: Complete LinkedIn Overhaul

Viết lại TOÀN BỘ LinkedIn profile: Headline + About + 2 Experience descriptions. Consistent tone across all sections.

Yêu cầu C1:
  • Headline: 80-120 characters, hook-y
  • About: 1200-1500 characters, 5 paragraphs
  • 2 Experience descriptions: specific, impact-focused
  • Consistent tone across all sections

📊 6-Criteria Writing Rubric

6 tiêu chí để self-assess LinkedIn writing. Click vào từng tab để xem chi tiết. Sau mỗi iteration, score từng criteria (1-5).

🎣
Hook Strength (20%)
Câu đầu tiên có khiến reader đọc tiếp không?
Score Description
5/5 Hook mạnh (story/question/bold statement). Reader phải đọc câu thứ 2. Memorable.
4/5 Hook tốt nhưng hơi generic. Reader vẫn đọc tiếp.
3/5 Hook OK nhưng không memorable. Reader có thể skip.
2/5 Hook yếu. Bắt đầu với "I am a..." hoặc generic statement.
1/5 Không có hook. Boring opener.
✅ Checklist
  • Câu đầu có phải story, question, hoặc bold statement không?
  • Reader có curiosity gap không?
  • Hook có memorable không?
🎯
Specificity (20%)
Có đủ numbers, names, places không?
Score Description
5/5 Nhiều specific details (5+). Numbers, names, places. Concrete, memorable.
4/5 Có specific details (3-4). Khá concrete.
3/5 Một số specific details (1-2). Hơi vague.
2/5 Ít specific details. Mostly vague statements.
1/5 Không có specific details. Hoàn toàn generic.
✅ Checklist
  • Có ít nhất 3 specific details không?
  • Có numbers (deals closed, $ amounts, years)?
  • Có names (companies, people)?
  • Có places (countries, cities)?
⚖️
Register Balance (15%)
Balance giữa professional và personality?
Score Description
5/5 Perfect balance. Professional nhưng vẫn có personality. Conversational.
4/5 Balance tốt nhưng hơi nghiêng về 1 side.
3/5 Balance OK nhưng hơi robotic hoặc hơi casual.
2/5 Quá formal (robotic) hoặc quá casual (unprofessional).
1/5 Hoàn toàn robotic hoặc hoàn toàn casual.
✅ Checklist
  • Đọc aloud nghe natural không?
  • Có personality touch không?
  • Có quá formal ("I am a seasoned professional") không?
  • Có quá casual ("Hey there!") không?
📐
Structure (15%)
Structure có clear, logical không?
Score Description
5/5 Structure rõ ràng (5 paragraphs). Flow smooth. Transitions natural.
4/5 Structure tốt nhưng 1-2 transitions hơi abrupt.
3/5 Structure OK nhưng flow không smooth.
2/5 Structure không rõ. Paragraphs không liên kết.
1/5 Không có structure. Random sentences.
✅ Checklist
  • Có 5 paragraphs không?
  • Paragraph 1: Hook?
  • Paragraph 2: What you do?
  • Paragraph 3: Why you do it?
  • Paragraph 4: Value proposition?
  • Paragraph 5: CTA + Personality?
  • Transitions smooth không?
💎
Value Proposition (15%)
Reader có biết bạn offer gì không?
Score Description
5/5 Value proposition clear, specific, compelling. Reader biết nên connect vì sao.
4/5 Value proposition clear nhưng hơi generic.
3/5 Value proposition OK nhưng không compelling.
2/5 Value proposition vague hoặc missing.
1/5 Không có value proposition.
✅ Checklist
  • Reader có biết bạn offer gì không?
  • Value proposition có specific không?
  • Reader có reason để connect không?
📣
Call to Action (15%)
Reader có biết nên làm gì tiếp không?
Score Description
5/5 CTA clear, specific, actionable. Reader biết nên làm gì.
4/5 CTA clear nhưng hơi generic.
3/5 CTA OK nhưng không compelling.
2/5 CTA vague hoặc missing.
1/5 Không có CTA.
✅ Checklist
  • Có CTA không?
  • CTA có specific không? (không chỉ "Let's connect")
  • CTA có actionable không?
  • Reader có biết nên làm gì tiếp không?

💡 Improvement Tips by Criteria

Click vào từng criteria để xem specific tips cho improvement. Nếu score < 3/5 ở criteria nào, xem tips tương ứng.

🎣 Hook Score < 3/5 +
🎯 Vấn đề

Câu đầu boring, generic, không khiến reader đọc tiếp.

💡 Giải pháp
  • Thay "I am..." bằng story opener: "Five years ago, I..."
  • Hoặc question hook: "What if I told you..."
  • Hoặc bold statement: "Most X are Y. We're not."
  • Test: Nếu câu đầu không khiến bạn đọc câu 2, rewrite
🎯 Practice
  • Viết 3 câu đầu khác nhau
  • Đọc aloud, chọn câu nào engaging nhất
  • Target: Câu đầu phải có curiosity gap
🎯 Specificity Score < 3/5 +
🎯 Vấn đề

Writing quá vague, không có numbers, names, places.

💡 Giải pháp
  • Thêm numbers: "12 deals worth $200M+" thay vì "many deals"
  • Thêm names: "Shopee, Grab, TikTok" thay vì "top companies"
  • Thêm places: "Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia" thay vì "Southeast Asia"
  • Thêm time: "Over the past 8 years" thay vì "For many years"
🎯 Practice
  • Highlight tất cả vague statements trong writing
  • Replace mỗi vague statement với specific detail
  • Target: ≥3 specific details trong About section
⚖️ Register Score < 3/5 +
🎯 Vấn đề

Quá formal (robotic) hoặc quá casual (unprofessional).

💡 Giải pháp
  • Đọc aloud - nếu nghe như robot → thêm personality
  • Nếu nghe quá casual → replace slang với professional phrases
  • Thêm personality touch ở cuối: hobby, value statement, quirky detail
  • Test: Would YOU connect với người này?
🎯 Practice
  • Record giọng đọc writing của mình
  • Listen back: Nghe natural không?
  • Adjust tone cho đến khi nghe như đang nói chuyện
📐 Structure Score < 3/5 +
🎯 Vấn đề

Không có 5 paragraphs, flow không smooth, transitions abrupt.

💡 Giải pháp
  • Follow 5-paragraph structure nghiêm túc
  • Thêm transition phrases: "Today, ...", "I believe...", "If you're..."
  • Đảm bảo mỗi paragraph có 1 purpose rõ ràng
  • Read aloud - nếu vấp ở đâu, đó là transition cần fix
🎯 Practice
  • Highlight purpose của mỗi paragraph
  • Check: Có đúng 5 paragraphs với 5 purposes không?
  • Read aloud, note vấp ở đâu → fix transition
💎 Value Prop Score < 3/5 +
🎯 Vấn đề

Reader không biết bạn offer gì, hoặc value proposition quá vague.

💡 Giải pháp
  • Follow structure: "I help [target] achieve [outcome] through [approach]"
  • Be specific: "logistics founders in SE Asia" thay vì "people"
  • Be specific: "raise their series A" thay vì "succeed"
  • Test: Reader có reason để connect không?
🎯 Practice
  • Viết value proposition theo structure
  • Ask friend: "Nếu đọc câu này, bạn có muốn connect không?"
  • Adjust cho đến khi câu trả lời là "Yes"
📣 CTA Score < 3/5 +
🎯 Vấn đề

CTA quá generic ("Let's connect") hoặc không có CTA.

💡 Giải pháp
  • Be specific: "Let's connect if you're [specific criteria]"
  • Be actionable: "DM me", "Grab coffee", "Check out my newsletter"
  • Combine với personality: "Always happy to chat about X over coffee — DM me!"
  • Test: Reader có biết nên làm gì tiếp không?
🎯 Practice
  • Viết 3 CTA khác nhau
  • Chọn CTA nào specific + actionable nhất
  • Target: Reader biết nên connect vì sao và như thế nào
