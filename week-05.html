Strategic Workplace Communication

Week 5 không phải về meeting English. Week 5 là về professional presence - khiến bạn được lắng nghe, được tôn trọng, và có sức ảnh hưởng. Meeting chỉ là bối cảnh - strategic communication mới là điều quan trọng.

"B2 speakers use meeting English. C1 speakers demonstrate professional presence."

Week 5 Philosophy

From Meeting English to Professional Presence

Phase 1 đã dạy bạn introduce, describe, và build relationships. Phase 2 sẽ dạy bạn lead, influence, và navigate complex professional situations. Week 5 tập trung vào strategic communication - biết khi nào nên lên tiếng, cách frame ý tưởng, và xử lý bất đồng một cách ngoại giao. Đây là sự khác biệt giữa participation và presence.

Small Talk vs Professional Presence
Small Talk (Surface level)
"I did X, Y, Z this week. I think we should do this. I disagree with that. I need help."
Professional Presence (C1 style)
"We've made significant progress on X, which unblocks Y. Building on what Sarah said, there's an opportunity here to... I see the merit in that, however my concern is... To ensure we hit the Q3 target, I'd like to realign our resources..."

7 Days of Strategic Communication

Mỗi ngày tập trung vào 1 khía cạnh của professional presence. Không phải meeting English - mà là strategic communication.

DAY 29
Executive Updates
Communicate impact, not just activity. Master executive updates.
Key Patterns:
  • "We've made significant progress on..."
  • "The key impact has been..."
  • "One risk to flag is..."
  • "Here's what I'd recommend..."
  • "Next steps are..."
60 min
DAY 30
Speaking Up & Interjecting
Claim space in meetings gracefully without being rude.
Key Patterns:
  • "Building on what [Name] just said..."
  • "Can I jump in here?"
  • "I'd like to add something..."
  • "If I could just weigh in..."
  • "To play devil's advocate..."
60 min
DAY 31
Framing Ideas
Frame ideas so others want to listen before you dive into details.
Key Patterns:
  • "There's an opportunity here to..."
  • "What if we approached this from..."
  • "The way I see it..."
  • "Here's the bigger picture..."
  • "Imagine if we could..."
60 min
DAY 32
Diplomatic Pushback
Handle disagreement with grace. Push back while protecting relationships.
Key Patterns:
  • "I see the merit in that, however..."
  • "My concern with that approach is..."
  • "I understand where you're coming from, but..."
  • "That's a valid point. At the same time..."
  • "Let me offer a different perspective..."
60 min
DAY 33
Managing Up & Expectations
Report to senior management and request support strategically.
Key Patterns:
  • "To ensure we hit [target], I'd like to..."
  • "The timeline may need adjusting because..."
  • "We're likely to miss [deadline] unless..."
  • "Here's what I'd recommend..."
  • "I'd like to propose a realignment of..."
60 min
DAY 34
Constructive Feedback
Give feedback that develops potential, not just points out mistakes.
Key Patterns:
  • "I noticed a gap in..."
  • "One area for growth might be..."
  • "Let's figure out how to streamline..."
  • "What would help you succeed here is..."
  • "I'd love to see more of..."
60 min
DAY 35
Week 5 Review
Integrate all 6 days into one complete professional communication playbook.
Focus:
  • Review all 30+ professional patterns
  • Integrated practice scenarios
  • Self-assessment & reflection
  • Celebrate Milestone 5
60 min
