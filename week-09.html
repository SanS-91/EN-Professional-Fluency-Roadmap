Week 9 Philosophy

4 Organizational Intelligence Principles

Week 5-8 đã dạy bạn **communicate professionally**. Week 9 dạy bạn **understand organizations** - framework tư duy để navigate complex organizational dynamics. Bốn nguyên tắc này sẽ xuyên suốt 7 ngày và thay đổi cách bạn đọc bối cảnh tổ chức.

PRINCIPLE 1
Map before moving
Hiểu landscape trước khi hành động. Know who has power, who has influence, who has stakes, và tại sao.
PRINCIPLE 2
Coalition before confrontation
Xây dựng liên minh trước khi đối đầu. Never go alone into difficult organizational dynamics.
PRINCIPLE 3
Momentum before perfection
Tạo đà tiến trước khi cầu toàn. Small wins build credibility for big organizational changes.
PRINCIPLE 4
Context before content
Hiểu bối cảnh trước khi truyền đạt nội dung. The same message delivered in different contexts has completely different impact.
Reactive vs Strategic Organizational Navigation
Reactive (B2 approach)
"I'll deal with issues as they come up. I need the CEO to approve this. People are resisting - we need to push harder."
Strategic (C1 approach)
"Let me map the stakeholders first. The CEO approves, but the CFO can veto. Resistance tells me what people are afraid of - let me understand the underlying concerns."

7 Days of Organizational Intelligence

Mỗi ngày xây dựng một khía cạnh của organizational intelligence. Từ stakeholder mapping đến reading the room.

DAY 57
Stakeholder Mapping & Power Dynamics
Map stakeholders cho organizational transformation. Understand formal vs informal power structures.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Who needs to be involved in this decision?"
  • "What matters most to [Name] in this situation?"
  • "I'd like to get your perspective before we move forward..."
  • "Based on what you've shared, I think we should..."
  • "Let's bring [Name] into this conversation because..."
60 min
DAY 58
Decision Landscape
Understand ai thực sự quyết định. Map decision makers, influencers, veto powers, và approval processes.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Who has the authority to approve this?"
  • "Who can influence this decision?"
  • "Who has veto power over this?"
  • "What's the approval process for this type of decision?"
  • "Who needs to be informed after the decision?"
60 min
DAY 59
Organizational Resistance
Understand tại sao people resist change. Resistance is data - listen to understand underlying concerns.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Help me understand what's concerning you about..."
  • "What would need to be true for this to work for you?"
  • "I hear that you're worried about [X]. Here's how we'd address that..."
  • "What would success look like from your perspective?"
  • "Let's find a way to make this work for everyone..."
60 min
DAY 60
Building Informal Influence
Build influence mà không có formal authority. Create credibility, reciprocity, và network of allies.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I've been working with [Name] on this, and they shared..."
  • "Based on my experience with [similar situation]..."
  • "I'd love to get your perspective on this because..."
  • "What if we approached it this way..."
  • "Let me connect you with [Name] who has expertise in..."
60 min
DAY 61
Strategic Alignment
Align priorities, interests, và expectations across organization. Create shared ownership.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Here's how your team's work connects to the bigger picture..."
  • "What do you need from other teams to succeed?"
  • "Let's make sure we're all working toward the same outcome..."
  • "Your expertise in [area] is critical to this..."
  • "How can we support each other to make this happen?"
60 min
DAY 62
Reading the Room
Learn khi nào push, stop, ask, hoặc stay silent. Read verbal và non-verbal signals trong organizational dynamics.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I'm sensing some hesitation. Let me pause and understand..."
  • "I notice the energy has shifted. What's coming up for people?"
  • "Before we move forward, let me check in..."
  • "I think we need to spend more time on this..."
  • "Let me step back and listen for a moment..."
60 min
DAY 63
Week 9 Review
Mô phỏng toàn bộ organizational intelligence journey. Apply all 6 days trong one complete simulation.
Focus:
  • Complete organizational intelligence simulation
  • Apply 4 principles in real-time
  • Self-assessment & reflection
  • Celebrate Milestone 9
60 min
