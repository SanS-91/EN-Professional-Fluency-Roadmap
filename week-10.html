Week 10 Philosophy

4 Channel Mastery Principles

Week 5-9 đã dạy bạn **communicate professionally**. Week 10 dạy bạn **master the channels** - framework tư duy để chọn đúng kênh, đúng thời điểm, và adapt message cho từng context. Bốn nguyên tắc này sẽ xuyên suốt 7 ngày và thay đổi cách bạn thiết kế communication.

PRINCIPLE 1
Right message. Right channel. Right timing.
Chọn kênh phù hợp với tính chất thông điệp. Rich message → rich channel. Simple message → lean channel.
PRINCIPLE 2
Reduce unnecessary communication
Mặc định dùng async, chỉ dùng sync khi thực sự cần. Tôn trọng thời gian của người khác.
PRINCIPLE 3
Write once. Communicate clearly.
Trong written communication, clarity quan trọng hơn style. Ambiguity là kẻ thù của remote work.
PRINCIPLE 4
Think from the receiver's perspective
Luôn hỏi: "What does my audience need to know, feel, and do?" Context is king.

Reactive vs Strategic Communication
Reactive (B2 approach)
"I'll just email everyone about this. Let's have a meeting to discuss. I'll reply to whatever comes in."
Strategic (C1 approach)
"This deserves a conversation, not an email. I've documented this so anyone in any timezone can pick it up. Let me design the communication flow for this initiative."

7 Days of Channel Mastery

Mỗi ngày xây dựng một khía cạnh của channel mastery. Từ channel selection đến communication architecture design.

DAY 64
Channel Selection & Media Richness
Hiểu framework để chọn channel phù hợp cho từng loại message. Rich message → rich channel.
Key Frameworks:
  • "This deserves a conversation, not an email"
  • "Let me put this in writing so we have a record"
  • "Can we jump on a quick call? This is easier to discuss live"
  • "I'll send a brief - no need to meet unless we need alignment"
  • "Let me choose the right channel for this message"
60 min
DAY 65
Synchronous vs Asynchronous Communication
Master the art of knowing when to interrupt và khi nào để people respond on their time.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I've documented this in a brief - please review when you have time"
  • "Let's meet only if we need real-time discussion"
  • "No need to respond immediately - this is for your awareness"
  • "Async by default, sync by design"
  • "The brief before meeting principle"
60 min
DAY 66
Channel Adaptation
Viết cùng một message nhưng khác nhau trên Email, Teams, Slack, WhatsApp, Documentation.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Subject: Decision needed on X by Friday. Context: [3 sentences]. Ask: [specific]."
  • "BLUF: We need to decide X by Friday"
  • "Context → Ask → Deadline structure"
  • "How to say 'no' gracefully in writing"
  • "Tone calibration: formal ↔ casual"
60 min
DAY 67
Real-time Communication
Navigate live interactions (calls, video, chat) mà không có body language đầy đủ.
Key Frameworks:
  • "The purpose of this call is to decide X. By the end, we need Y."
  • "Let me make sure I understand - are you saying...?"
  • "Before we hang up, let me confirm next steps..."
  • "Managing turn-taking in virtual settings"
  • "When to switch from chat → call → video"
60 min
DAY 68
Presenting Across Channels
Cùng một thông tin nhưng đổi cách truyền đạt: Slide → Email → Video → Meeting → Chat.
Key Frameworks:
  • "Let me show you this visually - it's easier to see than explain"
  • "The key insight here is [one thing]"
  • "I've recorded a 3-minute walkthrough you can watch at your convenience"
  • "One idea per slide principle"
  • "Visual storytelling in async contexts"
60 min
DAY 69
Cross-timezone & Distributed Communication
Communicate effectively across time zones và distributed teams. Design for global collaboration.
Key Frameworks:
  • "I've documented this so the team in [timezone] can pick it up"
  • "Timezone-aware scheduling"
  • "Designing for async-first global collaboration"
  • "Building rapport across distance"
  • "Avoiding misunderstandings in distributed teams"
60 min
DAY 70
Week 10 Review - The Channel Master
Mô phỏng toàn bộ channel mastery journey. Apply all 6 days trong one complete simulation.
Focus:
  • Complete channel mastery simulation
  • Apply 4 principles in real-time
  • Design communication architecture cho team/project
  • Celebrate Milestone 10
60 min
